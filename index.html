About us

Kalibandulu was founded in Milan in 1999 by MC and Deejay Rebel T and in a short time has established itself as one of the most representative sounds of Reggae and Dancehall music in the whole peninsula. Over the years the sound expands and grows quickly coming to tread the stages throughout Italy and Europe with unique Clash and parties of their kind becoming a solid point of reference for the whole scene. In the second decade they started international collaborations of the caliber of Major Lazer that bring the sound overseas to the States. On the national front they organize more and more parties, mainly in Milan and Salento bringing international Reggae artists to Italy.
